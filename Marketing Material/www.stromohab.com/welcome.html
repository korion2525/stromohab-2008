StroMoHab - Stroke Mobility Rehabilitation is being developed by MedTred at the University of York's Department of Electronics in the UK. StroMoHab is a treadmill based system that combines motion capture technology with virtual reality to aid in the rehabilitation of stroke patients.

Patients gain visual and auditory feedback from their movements and can practice real world tasks without the risk of hurting themselves. The clinician gains key diagnostic metrics and can track a patient’s recovery over a number of session as well as having access to an advanced task editor enabling them to ensure every session is tailored to the patients needs.

By using proprietary software running on mostly off-the-shelf hardware components, StroMoHab is able to provide advanced rehabilitation for stroke patients at a competitive hospital friendly price.
